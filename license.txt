ArControl (Arduino Controler) is an Arduino (UNO Rev3) real-time platform 
for inputs detection and outputs stimulation. Academic researchers using ArControl
should cite the paper in their works.

- Chen, X., and Li, H. (2017). ArControl: An Arduino-Based Comprehensive Behavioral 
Platform with Real-Time Performance. Front. Behav. Neurosci. 11, 244. 
doi: 10.3389/fnbeh.2017.00244.
